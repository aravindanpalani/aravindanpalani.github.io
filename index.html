Welcome to Little Krishna Montessori school

The main features of Little Krishna are Therapy consulting regularly for all the children and sharing reports to the parents and taking special care of the children.
Considering the health hygiene, lunch and snacks are provided to the children as per nutrition chart.
To implant the children’s mind about the importance of farming, Field trips and Organic Gardening by children is made mandatory.
Each class consists of only twenty-five students under the supervision of two teachers and one mentor to take the children.
New way of teacher training such as Montessori training with practical training is provided.
A sports academy with all necessary basic features is provided.
